اِقْرَأْ بِاسْمِ رَبِّكَ
Sadaqah Jariyah · صدقة جارية
إِهْدَاءً إِلَى رُوحَيْ
Badar Bin Easa&Hasan Bin Badar

O Allah, forgive them and have mercy on them, keep them safe and sound and pardon them, honor their rest and make their entrance wide.

O Allah, grant them Your Rahm, ease their journey in the Afterlife, and protect them from the trials of the grave through Your infinite mercy and help.

O Allah, make every letter read from this Mushaf a source of light for their souls, a companion for them in their graves, and a witness that intercedes for their entry into Al-Firdaus Al-A'lā.
